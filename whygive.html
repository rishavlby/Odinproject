why give

With your help, we can transform specialized adult health care and research in BC.


Vital is the important work of VGH & UBC Hospital Foundation, and it’s the crucial link that makes good health care better. We will reduce wait times, provide more targeted care for the vulnerable, enable better outcomes to those in critical condition, and improve the health care experience. We will only accomplish this with support from our donors—vital partners in transforming health care for all British Columbians.”
Reasons to Give Our services are vital to British Columbians: we are an adult’s best hope for restoring and maintaining good health The Foundation is vital to supporting that hope: we are leading the charge in transforming health care to ensure our system is sustainable for generations to come for all British Columbians who need us.
